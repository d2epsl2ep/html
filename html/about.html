About

Hello, I'm M. I'm a writer and developer who believes in the power of simplicity. This blog is where I share my thoughts on code, design, literature, and life — all in black and white.

I started this blog as a personal project — a quiet corner of the internet where I can write freely without the noise of social media. Here, I explore ideas that matter to me, document what I learn, and practice the craft of writing.

When I'm not writing, you'll find me reading, taking black-and-white photographs, or working on open-source projects. I'm drawn to minimalism in all its forms — clean code, simple designs, and honest writing.

This blog is built with pure HTML and CSS, deployed on GitHub Pages. It's intentionally simple — no frameworks, no JavaScript frameworks, no tracking. Just words on a page, the way it should be.

If you'd like to reach out, feel free to find me on GitHub or Twitter.
